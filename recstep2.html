2.
MAKE THE DOUGH.
Mix water, yeast, salt, sugar and oil together until dissolved. Add in the flour and mix with a spatula until incorporated. Cover the bowl with a warm wet cloth and let it sit at room temperature for about an hour or until the dough has doubled in size.
