Long days by the pool or beach clearly demand a Pina Colada, but it's time to give the classic drink a serious upgrade. This is a special cocktail created to transport your mind to a place far from ordinary life. Pineapple, strawberries, and peaches combine with tropics-approved ingredients like lime juice, coconut, and rum to make the cocktail version of an island sunset.
