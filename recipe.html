Patbingsu (Korean Shaved Ice) Recipe

I remember pretty clearly the day when my mom made a patbingsu for the first time at home.
It was the hottest summer and My sister and I needed something cold to a refreshing.
Patbingsu was our lifesaver and we can never forget the taste.
It's still my favorite dessert and it’s simple and satisfying.


    INGREDIENTS FOR PATBINGSU
  • Ice ( 1 tray / bowl )
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Sweetened Red Bean Paste
  • Fruit Cocktail
  • Mochi
  • Ice Cream ( whichever flavor you'd like )
  • Shaved Ice Machine

    RECIPE
  1. Using a electric shaved ice machine, fill a bowl with shaved ice and add condensed milk.
  2. Top with the red bean, mochi or one scoop of your favorite ice cream and add Fruit cocktail for fun.
  3. Get ready to mix!
